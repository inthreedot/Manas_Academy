About us.

Manas Academy, a Private Sector educational institute, situated at Kaljhar, that enjoys a unique place in the history of education, culture, art and socio-economic development of Assam. Manas Academy, run by Manas Rural Development Association, an NGO which is dedicated to the quality of education and social welfare duly registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 (No. BAR/237/Z/33 of 2020-2021) has set a high academic record and an example of excellence within a very short span of time.

The institute provides seven specific branches of formal education -

1) Manas Academy Assamese Medium School (Ankur to Astam Shreni).

2) Manas Academy Senior Secondary School (Class-IX, Class-X)

Manas Academy is a humble venture to generate an academic atmosphere for practical learning in the field of education and self-orientation programme. The Academy keeps in record the hurdles experienced in executing its honest and ambitious plans to guide the young learners and feels the same soluble with the active co-operation of the learners as well as the guardians. The practical and scientific basis of the teaching pedagogy of the Academy will assuredly yield fruitful crop with the learners, teachers and guardians partaking the spirit acknowledged. Manas Academy belongs to you, to us all and sundry. Let the enlightenment of Manas Academy ever kindle the light in you and all.

MISSION & VISION

Mission

MANAS ACADEMY, KALJHAR regards education as a mission to serve the people and nation at large by educating and molding students to take up responsibility willingly and voluntarily with all their commitment and social accountability expected of them in return of what the government is spending on them.

Vision

The holistic development of the rural and interior youth of areas. To make good quality higher education within the reach of the common people of Manas Academy, Kaljhar village and many surrounding villages in the region and to ensure that the education imparted would not only enrich the student's knowledge but also self discipline, self confidence and human values which would help them to become responsible citizen of the country.
