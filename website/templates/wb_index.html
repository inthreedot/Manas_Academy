About Manas Academy

It is a Private Sector educational institute situated at Kaljhar, that enjoys a unique place in the history of education, culture, art and socio-economic development of Assam. Manas Academy, run by Manas Rural Development Association, an NGO which is dedicated to the quality of education and social welfare duly registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860 (No. BAR/237/Z/33 of 2020-2021) has set a high academic record and an example of excellence within a very short span of time.
